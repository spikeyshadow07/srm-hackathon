EduGuard AI

Early Warning System for Student Dropout Prevention

EduGuard is an AI-powered platform designed for government schools to identify, monitor, and support students at risk of dropping out. The system combines multi-factor risk analytics with evidence-based interventions to help educators act early and keep every child in school.

  • Real-time risk scoring — Attendance, academic performance, socioeconomic factors, and behavioral indicators
  • Actionable insights — AI recommendations for targeted interventions
  • District-wide analytics — School-level dashboards and trend reports
  • Intervention tracking — Home visits, remedial support, counseling, and more

🤖 System Overview

This AI Early Warning System uses a Weighted Multi-Factor Risk Model to identify students at risk of dropping out from government schools. The model evaluates six key dimensions:


Attendance (30%)
Primary dropout predictor. Below 60% triggers critical alert.
Academic Performance (25%)
Failing grades compound attendance issues.
Socioeconomic Status (20%)
BPL families face highest financial pressure.
Distance to School (10%)
Over 10km significantly increases risk.
Family Background (10%)
Parental education, engagement, prior dropout history.
Behavioral Indicators (5%)
Disruptive behavior, absenteeism patterns.
